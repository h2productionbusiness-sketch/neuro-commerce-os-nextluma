# Higher Education Doctrine
business_type: higher-education
sector: Education & Training

## Visual Doctrine

### Colors
- primary: "#0F2A5F"
- secondary: "#F4F1E9"
- accent: "#A6192E"
- psychology: Collegiate navy signals institutional gravitas and trust, parchment cream evokes scholarly heritage, and crimson accents borrow the pageantry of academic tradition and achievement.

### Typography
- heading: Freight Display (Serif)
- body: Source Sans Pro (Sans-serif)
- psychology: A stately transitional serif conveys centuries of academic authority while a legible humanist sans reassures prospective students that the institution is modern and accessible.

### Imagery
- style: Documentary campus photography with warm natural light and candid moments.
- subjects: Diverse students in seminars, historic architecture, labs, quads at golden hour, and graduation regalia.
- psychology: Images should trigger aspiration, belonging, and the pride of joining a lasting community of scholars.

### Layout
- style: Structured grid with prominent hero imagery, program cards, and clear informational hierarchy.
- white_space: Moderate
- psychology: Ordered structure signals institutional competence and helps anxious applicants navigate a high-stakes decision with confidence.

### UI Patterns
- navigation: Persistent mega-menu organized by audience (prospective, current, alumni) and program area.
- cta_style: Confident solid buttons ("Apply Now", "Request Info", "Visit Campus") in accent color with supporting secondary links.
- psychology: Clear directive CTAs reduce decision paralysis in a complex, multi-step enrollment journey.

### Copy Style
- tone: Authoritative yet warm, outcome-focused and inclusive.
- length: Medium
- psychology: Balancing prestige with reassurance addresses both the aspiration to belong and the fear of not fitting in or affording it.
