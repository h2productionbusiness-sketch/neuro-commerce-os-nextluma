# Hotels & Hospitality Doctrine
business_type: hospitality
sector: Other Verticals

## Visual Doctrine

### Colors
- primary: "#1C2B3A"
- secondary: "#F4EDE4"
- accent: "#B08D57"
- psychology: Twilight navy evokes restful escape and premium calm, warm sand conveys welcoming comfort, and soft brass signals refined indulgence and elevated service.

### Typography
- heading: Cormorant Garamond (Serif)
- body: Josefin Sans (Sans-serif)
- psychology: Elegant high-contrast serif headlines evoke destination romance and refinement while an airy geometric body reads modern and effortless.

### Imagery
- style: Immersive, aspirational photography with golden light and a sense of place.
- subjects: Suites and lobbies, poolside and views, curated dining, guests relaxing, and local scenery.
- psychology: Images should trigger escapism, indulgence, and the anticipation of a memorable experience.

### Layout
- style: Full-bleed visual layout with immersive imagery and a prominent booking widget.
- white_space: Generous
- psychology: Spacious, cinematic design lets guests imagine themselves there and makes booking feel like the reward.

### UI Patterns
- navigation: Minimal top nav (Rooms, Dining, Experiences, Book) with a persistent date-and-rate booking bar.
- cta_style: Elegant solid CTAs ("Check Availability", "Reserve Your Stay") that feel inviting and effortless.
- psychology: Seamless booking flow reduces friction at the moment desire peaks, capturing intent instantly.

### Copy Style
- tone: Evocative, warm, experience-led.
- length: Medium
- psychology: Sensory storytelling about escape and indulgence sells the feeling, not just the room.
