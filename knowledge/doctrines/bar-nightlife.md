# Bars & Nightlife Doctrine
business_type: bar-nightlife
sector: Food & Beverage

## Visual Doctrine

### Colors
- primary: "#0D0A1F"
- secondary: "#1A1330"
- accent: "#FF2E9A"
- psychology: Midnight indigo-black sets the after-dark stage, deep violet adds mystery and depth, and electric neon magenta triggers dopamine-charged excitement and the pull of the night out.

### Typography
- heading: Monument Extended (Sans-serif)
- body: Space Grotesk (Sans-serif)
- psychology: Wide futuristic display type feels like a club marquee and flyer culture, projecting bold energy and cool-crowd belonging.

### Imagery
- style: High-contrast neon-lit nightlife photography with motion blur, crowds, and glowing cocktails.
- subjects: Packed dance floors, bartenders pouring flaming cocktails, DJ booths, and neon-drenched interiors.
- psychology: Images should trigger excitement, social energy, and fear of missing the night everyone's talking about.

### Layout
- style: Dark immersive layout with glowing accents, event calendar, and bold flyer-style sections.
- white_space: Minimal
- psychology: Dense neon-lit design mirrors the sensory overload of a great night out and keeps energy high.

### UI Patterns
- navigation: Sticky dark bar with Events, Tables, and Reserve, glowing on hover.
- cta_style: Glowing neon buttons ("Book a Table", "Get on the List", "Tonight's Lineup").
- psychology: Bright pulsing CTAs create urgency and exclusivity, mirroring line-outside, list-only nightlife culture.

### Copy Style
- tone: Bold, edgy, in-the-know.
- length: Short
- psychology: Punchy insider language makes readers feel they belong to the cool crowd and must show up tonight.
