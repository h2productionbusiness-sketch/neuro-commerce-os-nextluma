# QSR (Quick Service Restaurant) Doctrine
business_type: food-qsr
sector: Food & Beverage

## Visual Doctrine

### Colors
- primary: "#D62300"
- secondary: "#FFC72C"
- accent: "#1E1E1E"
- psychology: Appetite-stimulating red drives urgency and hunger cues while golden yellow signals speed, value, and instant gratification.

### Typography
- heading: Anton (Sans-serif, condensed display)
- body: Nunito Sans (Sans-serif)
- psychology: Bold condensed headlines shout value deals in a split second while rounded body text keeps the brand friendly and approachable.

### Imagery
- style: Hyper-saturated close-up food photography with steam, drips, and cheese pulls shot at eye level.
- subjects: Hero product shots, combo meals, hands grabbing food, drive-thru moments.
- psychology: Trigger immediate salivation and craving so the viewer acts on impulse rather than deliberation.

### Layout
- style: High-density grid with big price callouts and limited-time-offer banners.
- white_space: Minimal
- psychology: Visual abundance signals value and speed, pushing fast low-consideration decisions.

### UI Patterns
- navigation: Sticky bottom order bar with one-tap menu categories.
- cta_style: Oversized red "Order Now" buttons with countdown timers on deals.
- psychology: Reduce friction to near zero because every extra tap loses a hungry impulse buyer.

### Copy Style
- tone: Punchy, playful, deal-driven.
- length: Short
- psychology: Hungry customers skim, so three-word hooks and price anchors beat any paragraph.
