# Real Estate Doctrine
business_type: real-estate
sector: Real Estate & Construction

## Visual Doctrine

### Colors
- primary: "#12333B"
- secondary: "#F7F3EC"
- accent: "#C6A15B"
- psychology: Deep evergreen signals rooted stability and the security of home, warm cream conveys welcoming livability, and soft gold cues aspirational lifestyle and the emotional value of place.

### Typography
- heading: Playfair Display (Serif)
- body: Lato (Sans-serif)
- psychology: An elegant high-contrast serif evokes lifestyle aspiration and premium listings while a warm humanist body reads as approachable and personal.

### Imagery
- style: Aspirational lifestyle and twilight architectural photography with warm interiors and inviting curb appeal.
- subjects: Sunlit living spaces, families settling in, neighborhood scenes, and hero shots of signature listings.
- psychology: Images should trigger aspiration, belonging, and the emotional pull of imagining oneself already living there.

### Layout
- style: Listing-forward gallery grid with large hero imagery, saved-search paths, and neighborhood guides.
- white_space: Moderate
- psychology: Emotive full-bleed imagery leads while clean supporting structure keeps browsing effortless and desire-driven.

### UI Patterns
- navigation: Prominent search bar with Buy, Sell, and Neighborhoods plus a persistent agent-contact CTA.
- cta_style: Warm inviting buttons ("See This Home", "Find Your Home", "What's My Home Worth?") tied to an agent's face.
- psychology: Personal agent-anchored CTAs convert an emotional, relationship-driven purchase decision.

### Copy Style
- tone: Warm, aspirational, personal.
- length: Medium
- psychology: Emotive lifestyle language sells the feeling of home while the agent's presence builds the trust a life-changing purchase requires.
