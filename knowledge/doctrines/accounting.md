## Visual Doctrine

### Colors
- primary: "#14532D"
- secondary: "#F5F7F4"
- accent: "#2F6FEB"
- psychology: Grounded green signals ledgers, growth, and financial health, soft off-white reads as clean and precise, and a clear blue conveys competence and digital trust.

### Typography
- heading: GT Walsheim (Sans-serif)
- body: IBM Plex Sans (Sans-serif)
- psychology: Clean, methodical sans-serifs read as accurate and detail-oriented, reassuring clients that numbers are handled with care.

### Imagery
- style: Bright, orderly photography and clean data-driven illustrations and dashboards.
- subjects: Relieved business owners, organized workspaces, clear financial dashboards, and calendar/deadline motifs.
- psychology: Images should trigger relief, order, and confidence that finances are under control and compliant.

### Layout
- style: Clean modular layout with checklists, comparison tables, and clear service tiers.
- white_space: Moderate
- psychology: Tidy, well-organized layouts mirror the precision and organization clients want in their books.

### UI Patterns
- navigation: Simple service-based navigation with a persistent consultation and pricing path.
- cta_style: Clear solid CTAs ("Get a quote", "Book a call") with transparency about scope and fees.
- psychology: Straightforward, transparent interaction reduces the stress of handing over sensitive finances.

### Copy Style
- tone: Clear, competent, reassuring, deadline-aware.
- length: Medium
- psychology: Plain, precise language proves the firm removes complexity rather than adding to it.
