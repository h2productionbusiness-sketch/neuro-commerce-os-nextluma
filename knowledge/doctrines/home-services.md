# Home Services Doctrine
business_type: home-services
sector: Real Estate & Construction

## Visual Doctrine

### Colors
- primary: "#0E5A8A"
- secondary: "#FFFFFF"
- accent: "#FF6B35"
- psychology: Trustworthy service-blue signals licensed reliability and clean workmanship, crisp white conveys hygiene and professionalism, and high-energy orange drives urgent "book now" action for homeowners with an active problem.

### Typography
- heading: Montserrat (Sans-serif)
- body: Open Sans (Sans-serif)
- psychology: Bold sturdy headlines feel dependable and get-it-done while a plain readable body signals honest straightforward pricing.

### Imagery
- style: Bright real-job photography of uniformed technicians, before-and-after shots, and branded trucks.
- subjects: Smiling licensed techs, clean vans, completed installs, and reassured homeowners at the door.
- psychology: Images should trigger trust, relief, and the feeling that a competent professional will show up fast and fix it right.

### Layout
- style: Conversion-first landing layout with a sticky booking bar, trust badges, and prominent phone number.
- white_space: Minimal
- psychology: Dense above-the-fold proof and an ever-present call button capture homeowners in urgent buying mode before they bounce.

### UI Patterns
- navigation: Simple service-menu nav with click-to-call always visible and an instant online booking button.
- cta_style: Loud high-contrast buttons ("Book Same-Day Service", "Get a Free Quote") repeated throughout the page.
- psychology: Repeated urgent CTAs and a tap-to-call number convert homeowners at the peak of an emergency.

### Copy Style
- tone: Friendly, urgent, trustworthy.
- length: Short
- psychology: Fast reassuring language with licensing and guarantees removes the fear of hiring the wrong contractor.
