# Cafes & Coffee Shops Doctrine
business_type: cafe
sector: Food & Beverage

## Visual Doctrine

### Colors
- primary: "#4A3728"
- secondary: "#F3EBDD"
- accent: "#C67B4A"
- psychology: Rich espresso brown evokes warmth and roasted comfort, oat-milk cream feels cozy and approachable, and terracotta signals hand-crafted, community-rooted hospitality.

### Typography
- heading: Fraunces (Serif)
- body: Inter (Sans-serif)
- psychology: A friendly modern serif paired with a clean sans reads as artisanal-yet-accessible, the exact feeling of a neighborhood specialty coffee shop.

### Imagery
- style: Warm natural-light lifestyle photography with steam, latte art, and cozy interiors.
- subjects: Latte pours, pastries, baristas, laptop corners, and regulars chatting at communal tables.
- psychology: Images should trigger comfort, belonging, and the small daily joy of a ritual pause.

### Layout
- style: Warm grid with rounded cards, generous imagery, and a welcoming community feel.
- white_space: Moderate
- psychology: A relaxed but full layout mirrors the cozy-but-lively atmosphere that makes a cafe a third place.

### UI Patterns
- navigation: Simple top bar with Menu, Hours, Location, and Order Ahead prominent.
- cta_style: Rounded friendly buttons ("Order Ahead", "See Today's Bake") in warm accent tones.
- psychology: Approachable, low-friction actions match the casual habitual nature of a coffee run.

### Copy Style
- tone: Warm, casual, community-first.
- length: Short
- psychology: Friendly conversational copy makes the cafe feel like a neighbor rather than a chain, building daily loyalty.
