# Electric Vehicles & Charging Doctrine
business_type: ev-charging
sector: Automotive & Transport

## Visual Doctrine

### Colors
- primary: "#0A2E36"
- secondary: "#F4F7F5"
- accent: "#2EE6A6"
- psychology: Deep teal-green signals clean energy and forward-thinking calm, near-white conveys spotless efficiency, and electric mint-green sparks the feeling of live current, sustainability, and effortless charge.

### Typography
- heading: Space Grotesk (Sans-serif)
- body: Inter (Sans-serif)
- psychology: Geometric near-technical headlines feel engineered and future-facing while a clean humanist body keeps sustainability messaging approachable and credible.

### Imagery
- style: Bright airy photography with soft gradients, glowing charge indicators, and open sunlit landscapes.
- subjects: Sleek EVs charging, illuminated connectors clicking in, app dashboards, and effortless daily moments plugged in at home.
- psychology: Images should trigger aspiration toward a cleaner future, calm confidence about range, and the satisfaction of a seamless charge.

### Layout
- style: Airy modern grid with animated energy-flow motifs and card-based station/plan info.
- white_space: Generous
- psychology: Open bright space communicates cleanliness, simplicity, and freedom from range anxiety.

### UI Patterns
- navigation: Map-first interface with live station availability and clear plan/pricing tabs.
- cta_style: Rounded glowing buttons ("Find a Charger", "Start Charging", "Book Install") with subtle energy animation.
- psychology: A live map and instant-start CTA replace uncertainty with real-time control, the core emotional need of EV drivers.

### Copy Style
- tone: Optimistic, clear, quietly progressive.
- length: Medium
- psychology: Reassuring precise language about range, speed, and cost dissolves the anxiety that blocks EV adoption.
