# Food Trucks Doctrine
business_type: food-truck
sector: Food & Beverage

## Visual Doctrine

### Colors
- primary: "#E23B2E"
- secondary: "#FFD23F"
- accent: "#2BB673"
- psychology: Bold appetite-red grabs attention on a busy street and stimulates hunger, sunny yellow radiates fun and energy, and fresh green signals quality and craveable flavor.

### Typography
- heading: Bebas Neue (Sans-serif)
- body: Poppins (Sans-serif)
- psychology: Loud condensed display type reads like street signage and menu boards, projecting bold personality that stands out at a glance from across a plaza.

### Imagery
- style: Vibrant high-saturation street-food photography with dripping cheese, char, and hand-held action shots.
- subjects: Signature dishes mid-bite, the truck window, sizzling grills, and happy crowds in line.
- psychology: Images should trigger instant hunger, fun, and fear-of-missing-out on a limited stop.

### Layout
- style: Punchy mobile-first layout with big menu photos, location map, and today's-schedule front and center.
- white_space: Minimal
- psychology: Dense energetic layout mirrors the fast, festive street-food vibe and rewards quick scanning on a phone.

### UI Patterns
- navigation: Sticky bottom bar with Menu, Find the Truck, and Order.
- cta_style: Big bright buttons ("Find Us Today", "Order Now") impossible to miss.
- psychology: Loud immediate CTAs match impulse-driven decisions made while hungry and on the move.

### Copy Style
- tone: Playful, punchy, high-energy.
- length: Short
- psychology: Snappy craveable copy drives fast impulse decisions before the customer walks to the next vendor.
