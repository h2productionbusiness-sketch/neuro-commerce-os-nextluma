# Music & Streaming Doctrine
business_type: music
sector: Entertainment & Media

## Visual Doctrine

### Colors
- primary: "#1A0B2E"
- secondary: "#F72585"
- accent: "#4CC9F0"
- psychology: Deep midnight purple evokes late-night listening and stage darkness, hot magenta mirrors the emotional intensity of sound, and electric cyan reads as sonic energy and playlist freshness.

### Typography
- heading: Clash Display (Sans-serif)
- body: Inter (Sans-serif)
- psychology: A bold expressive display face performs like an album cover shout while a clean neutral body keeps track lists and lyrics effortlessly scannable.

### Imagery
- style: High-contrast concert and studio photography with motion blur, gel lighting, and grain.
- subjects: Artists mid-performance, waveform art, vinyl and cassette textures, crowd silhouettes, and studio close-ups.
- psychology: Images should trigger anticipation, belonging to a scene, and the visceral rush of a live moment.

### Layout
- style: Card-based playlist grid with immersive full-bleed hero and horizontal scrolling shelves.
- white_space: Moderate
- psychology: A browsable shelf layout mimics the endless-discovery dopamine loop of streaming apps.

### UI Patterns
- navigation: Persistent bottom or sticky player bar with tab-based discovery navigation.
- cta_style: High-contrast pill buttons ("Listen Now", "Pre-Save", "Follow") with play-icon affordances.
- psychology: An ever-present play control removes friction between craving and gratification.

### Copy Style
- tone: Energetic, intimate, culturally fluent.
- length: Short
- psychology: Punchy fan-native phrasing signals the brand belongs to the culture rather than marketing at it.
