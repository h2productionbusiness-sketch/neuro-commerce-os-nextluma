# Commercial Real Estate Doctrine
business_type: real-estate-commercial
sector: Real Estate & Construction

## Visual Doctrine

### Colors
- primary: "#1B2A4A"
- secondary: "#E8EAEF"
- accent: "#B08D4C"
- psychology: Deep institutional navy signals fiduciary stability and boardroom trust, cool grey conveys analytical rigor, and brushed brass accents cue asset-class prestige to sophisticated investors.

### Typography
- heading: Canela (Serif)
- body: Inter (Sans-serif)
- psychology: A confident transitional serif reads as established and institutional while a neutral grotesque body signals data clarity and diligence.

### Imagery
- style: Architectural photography with wide-angle drone perspectives, twilight glass towers, and clean floorplan renders.
- subjects: Class-A towers, mixed-use developments, executives touring space, capital-stack diagrams, and skyline context shots.
- psychology: Images should trigger trust, aspiration for scale, and confidence that the asset is a durable long-term position.

### Layout
- style: Structured data-forward grid with prominent metrics, cap-rate callouts, and downloadable offering-memorandum blocks.
- white_space: Moderate
- psychology: Ordered density signals institutional competence — investors trust layouts that surface numbers without clutter.

### UI Patterns
- navigation: Persistent top bar with Listings, Investment Criteria, Track Record, and a gated deal-room login.
- cta_style: Solid authoritative buttons ("Request Offering Memorandum", "Schedule a Tour") paired with a broker direct line.
- psychology: Decisive high-commitment CTAs mirror the deliberate, high-ticket nature of commercial transactions.

### Copy Style
- tone: Authoritative, data-driven, fiduciary.
- length: Medium
- psychology: Precise numerically-anchored language reassures institutional buyers that returns are underwritten, not hyped.
