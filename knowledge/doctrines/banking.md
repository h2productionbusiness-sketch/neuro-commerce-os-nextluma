# Banking Doctrine
business_type: banking
sector: Financial & Legal

## Visual Doctrine

### Colors
- primary: "#0A2540"
- secondary: "#F4F6F9"
- accent: "#1E7A46"
- psychology: Deep navy conveys institutional stability and safekeeping, cool grey reads as clean and audited, and a grounded green signals prosperity and the movement of money.

### Typography
- heading: Söhne (Sans-serif)
- body: Inter (Sans-serif)
- psychology: Sturdy, unornamented sans-serifs read as transparent and dependable, reassuring customers that nothing is hidden in the fine print.

### Imagery
- style: Bright, high-key lifestyle photography and clean isometric product illustrations.
- subjects: Diverse real customers using apps, families reaching milestones, secure UI screens, and abstract data-flow graphics.
- psychology: Images should trigger trust, calm control over money, and optimism about the future.

### Layout
- style: Structured card-based grid with clear hierarchy and dashboard-like modules.
- white_space: Moderate
- psychology: Orderly, gridded layouts signal that the institution keeps your finances organized and under control.

### UI Patterns
- navigation: Persistent top and bottom nav with clear labels, account balances surfaced immediately.
- cta_style: Solid high-contrast primary buttons ("Open account", "Transfer") with visible security cues.
- psychology: Predictable, obvious navigation lowers anxiety when money is on the line.

### Copy Style
- tone: Clear, reassuring, plainspoken, compliance-aware.
- length: Medium
- psychology: Simple, jargon-free language builds trust by proving the bank has nothing to obscure.
