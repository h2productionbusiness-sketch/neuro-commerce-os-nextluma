# Catering & Events Food Doctrine
business_type: catering
sector: Food & Beverage

## Visual Doctrine

### Colors
- primary: "#2E3B4E"
- secondary: "#F7F4EF"
- accent: "#B08D57"
- psychology: Deep slate-navy conveys dependability and polished professionalism for high-stakes events, soft ivory feels elegant and clean, and champagne gold signals celebration and refined taste.

### Typography
- heading: Playfair Display (Serif)
- body: Lato (Sans-serif)
- psychology: An elegant celebratory serif over a trustworthy neutral sans balances the emotional occasion with the reliability event planners demand.

### Imagery
- style: Polished event photography with styled tablescapes, abundance, and warm celebratory lighting.
- subjects: Grazing tables, plated banquet service, staff in action, and joyful guests at weddings and galas.
- psychology: Images should trigger aspiration for a flawless event and trust that the day will be handled.

### Layout
- style: Structured portfolio-style layout with galleries, package tiers, and clear inquiry paths.
- white_space: Generous
- psychology: An organized, gallery-forward layout reassures planners that this vendor is meticulous and event-ready.

### UI Patterns
- navigation: Clear top bar with Portfolio, Menus, Packages, and Get a Quote.
- cta_style: Confident buttons ("Request a Quote", "Check Your Date") that move toward booking.
- psychology: Direct quote-focused CTAs match the considered, high-value nature of booking event catering.

### Copy Style
- tone: Warm, reassuring, professional.
- length: Medium
- psychology: Copy that blends celebration with logistical confidence eases planner anxiety and builds trust to book.
