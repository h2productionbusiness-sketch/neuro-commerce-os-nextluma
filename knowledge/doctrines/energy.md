## Visual Doctrine

### Colors
- primary: "#0A3D5C"
- secondary: "#EAF2F5"
- accent: "#2ECC71"
- psychology: Deep utility-blue signals stability, reliability, and grid-scale trust, clean off-white conveys transparency, and vivid green codes clean-energy progress and sustainability.

### Typography
- heading: Barlow (Sans-serif)
- body: Inter (Sans-serif)
- psychology: Confident engineered headlines feel modern and infrastructural while a highly legible body reads clear and accountable, matching a regulated, trust-critical sector.

### Imagery
- style: Expansive, optimistic photography of infrastructure and clean-energy landscapes.
- subjects: Solar arrays, wind farms, grid infrastructure, control rooms, and communities powered.
- psychology: Images should trigger trust in reliability, optimism about the future, and a sense of collective benefit.

### Layout
- style: Corporate, data-forward layout with clear savings calculators and reliability metrics.
- white_space: Moderate
- psychology: Transparent, metric-driven structure reassures buyers making high-stakes, long-horizon commitments.

### UI Patterns
- navigation: Clear top nav with segments (Residential, Business, Sustainability, Support) and prominent quote tools.
- cta_style: Reassuring solid CTAs ("Estimate My Savings", "Switch Today") emphasizing control and benefit.
- psychology: Simple, confidence-building interaction lowers anxiety around switching an essential service.

### Copy Style
- tone: Reassuring, transparent, benefit-led.
- length: Medium
- psychology: Clarity about savings, reliability, and sustainability reduces the perceived risk of a long-term commitment.
