# Restaurant Doctrine
business_type: restaurant
sector: Food & Beverage

## Visual Doctrine

### Colors
- primary: "#7A1F1F"
- secondary: "#FBF6EE"
- accent: "#E8A33D"
- psychology: Rich appetite-red stimulates hunger and warmth, creamy off-white feels clean and inviting like a fresh tablecloth, and golden amber evokes comfort food and a welcoming glow.

### Typography
- heading: Recoleta (Serif)
- body: Nunito Sans (Sans-serif)
- psychology: A soft rounded serif paired with a friendly sans reads as warm and welcoming, inviting families and regulars alike without feeling stuffy or cheap.

### Imagery
- style: Bright appetizing food photography with natural light, warm plating, and inviting dining spaces.
- subjects: Signature dishes, shareable plates, happy diners, and the dining-room atmosphere.
- psychology: Images should trigger hunger, comfort, and the anticipation of a good meal out with people you like.

### Layout
- style: Clean welcoming layout with hero dishes, menu highlights, and clear reservation and ordering paths.
- white_space: Moderate
- psychology: A balanced, easy-to-scan layout reassures diners the place is quality yet approachable, lowering the barrier to book or order.

### UI Patterns
- navigation: Clear top bar with Menu, Reservations, Order Online, and Location.
- cta_style: Warm inviting buttons ("Book a Table", "Order Online", "See the Menu").
- psychology: Clear, action-oriented CTAs match the practical decision of where to eat tonight.

### Copy Style
- tone: Warm, inviting, appetite-forward.
- length: Medium
- psychology: Craveable, welcoming copy about signature dishes builds appetite and makes the restaurant feel like the easy choice.
