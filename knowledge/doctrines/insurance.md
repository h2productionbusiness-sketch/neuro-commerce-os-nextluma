# Insurance Doctrine
business_type: insurance
sector: Financial & Legal

## Visual Doctrine

### Colors
- primary: "#123A6B"
- secondary: "#EEF3F8"
- accent: "#F2A93B"
- psychology: Steady mid-blue signals protection and dependability, soft pale blue reads as calm reassurance, and a warm amber conveys human care and the promise of being looked after.

### Typography
- heading: Tiempos (Serif)
- body: Source Sans Pro (Sans-serif)
- psychology: A humanist serif paired with a friendly sans balances the gravity of protection with approachable warmth, so customers feel safe rather than intimidated.

### Imagery
- style: Warm documentary lifestyle photography with natural light and genuine expressions.
- subjects: Families, homes, cars, small businesses, and real people at ease knowing they are covered.
- psychology: Images should trigger relief, security, and the emotional comfort of protecting what matters.

### Layout
- style: Guided step layout with clear sections, comparison tables, and progress indicators.
- white_space: Moderate
- psychology: A calm, guided structure reduces the overwhelm of complex coverage decisions.

### UI Patterns
- navigation: Linear quote-flow navigation with a persistent help path and transparent progress.
- cta_style: Reassuring solid CTAs ("Get my quote", "Protect my family") with trust badges nearby.
- psychology: Hand-holding interaction lowers fear and decision paralysis around risk.

### Copy Style
- tone: Empathetic, reassuring, clear about coverage, never fear-mongering.
- length: Medium
- psychology: Warm, transparent language turns anxiety about worst-case scenarios into confidence in being protected.
