# Pet Services Doctrine
business_type: pet-services
sector: Other Verticals

## Visual Doctrine

### Colors
- primary: "#2C6E6B"
- secondary: "#FBEEE0"
- accent: "#F7A072"
- psychology: Calming teal signals safety and professional care, warm cream conveys cozy comfort, and playful coral sparks the joy and affection owners feel toward their pets.

### Typography
- heading: Quicksand (Sans-serif)
- body: Nunito (Sans-serif)
- psychology: Rounded friendly headlines feel warm and approachable while a soft humanist body reads caring and trustworthy, matching an emotionally invested pet parent.

### Imagery
- style: Bright, candid lifestyle photography with joyful natural moments.
- subjects: Happy pets mid-play, groomers and sitters caring for animals, owners reuniting with pets, cozy facilities.
- psychology: Images should trigger joy, affection, and reassurance that a beloved pet is in loving, capable hands.

### Layout
- style: Friendly card-based layout with clear service menus, pricing, and booking front and center.
- white_space: Moderate
- psychology: Warm, easy-to-scan structure lowers hesitation and makes booking feel effortless and safe.

### UI Patterns
- navigation: Simple top nav (Services, Pricing, Book Now, Reviews) with an always-visible booking button.
- cta_style: Friendly rounded CTAs ("Book a Groom", "Meet Your Sitter") that feel warm and low-pressure.
- psychology: Inviting, frictionless interaction reflects the trust and gentleness owners want in a pet-care brand.

### Copy Style
- tone: Warm, playful, reassuring.
- length: Short
- psychology: Affectionate, pet-first language mirrors the owner's love and quickly builds emotional trust.
