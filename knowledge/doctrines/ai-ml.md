## Visual Doctrine

### Colors
- primary: "#7C3AED"
- secondary: "#0B0F1A"
- accent: "#00E0C6"
- psychology: Violet evokes intelligence and the near-magical frontier of new capability, deep space-black frames it as cutting-edge, and a teal-cyan glow signals living, generative energy.

### Typography
- heading: Space Grotesk (Sans-serif)
- body: Inter (Sans-serif)
- psychology: A slightly futuristic geometric display over a neutral body reads as innovative yet trustworthy — advanced without feeling alien.

### Imagery
- style: Generative gradients, particle fields, glowing neural motifs, and animated model outputs.
- subjects: Live demos, prompt-to-result transformations, and abstract representations of intelligence and data flow.
- psychology: Images should trigger awe and anticipation of superhuman capability while implying it is safe and controllable.

### Layout
- style: Immersive dark hero with interactive demo, capability grid, and benchmark proof sections.
- white_space: Generous
- psychology: Spacious dark canvases make the technology feel premium and futuristic, letting the glowing output be the hero.

### UI Patterns
- navigation: Minimal top nav with a live "Try the demo" CTA and a "Book a demo" enterprise path.
- cta_style: Glowing gradient buttons inviting hands-on trial ("See it in action", "Run a demo").
- psychology: Letting users experience the model directly converts wonder into belief faster than any claim.

### Copy Style
- tone: Visionary yet grounded, benefit-and-benchmark led.
- length: Medium
- psychology: Balancing bold capability claims with concrete metrics counters skepticism and hype fatigue.
