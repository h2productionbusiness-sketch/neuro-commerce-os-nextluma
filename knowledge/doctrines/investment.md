## Visual Doctrine

### Colors
- primary: "#10243E"
- secondary: "#F7F5F0"
- accent: "#B9975B"
- psychology: Midnight navy signals discretion and long-term stewardship, warm ivory reads as private and considered, and antique gold connotes accumulated wealth and quiet prestige.

### Typography
- heading: Canela (Serif)
- body: Graphik (Sans-serif)
- psychology: An elegant high-contrast serif over a precise modern sans projects sophistication and disciplined expertise, appealing to buyers who equate refinement with competence.

### Imagery
- style: Restrained editorial photography with muted tones, textured materials, and data visualizations.
- subjects: Considered portraits of advisors and clients, architectural spaces, charts framed as craft, and horizon imagery.
- psychology: Images should trigger aspiration, confidence in expertise, and a sense of belonging to a discerning circle.

### Layout
- style: Spacious editorial grid with generous margins and confident data modules.
- white_space: Generous
- psychology: Ample space signals that the firm is measured and unhurried — it manages wealth patiently, not frantically.

### UI Patterns
- navigation: Minimal top navigation with a private-client tone and clear performance and access entry points.
- cta_style: Understated but confident CTAs ("Book a consultation", "Review your portfolio") in refined tones.
- psychology: Calm, uncluttered interaction reinforces that decisions here are deliberate and expert-guided.

### Copy Style
- tone: Authoritative, discreet, long-horizon, evidence-led.
- length: Medium
- psychology: Measured, data-backed language signals competence and reassures clients their capital is in disciplined hands.
