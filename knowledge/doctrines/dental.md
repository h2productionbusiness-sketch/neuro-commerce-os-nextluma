# Dental & Orthodontics Doctrine
business_type: dental
sector: Health & Wellness

## Visual Doctrine

### Colors
- primary: "#1FA5C7"
- secondary: "#FBFDFE"
- accent: "#7DD4A8"
- psychology: Clean aqua-blue signals hygiene, freshness, and clinical precision, near-white brightness mirrors the promised bright smile, and a mint accent reinforces health and gentle care.

### Typography
- heading: Montserrat (Sans-serif)
- body: Open Sans (Sans-serif)
- psychology: Confident, tidy sans headlines convey competence and modern equipment while an open, friendly body face eases dental-anxiety.

### Imagery
- style: Bright, high-key photography of confident smiles, clean modern clinics, and reassuring staff.
- subjects: Genuine smiling patients, before/after smiles, aligners and clean treatment rooms, warm chairside interactions.
- psychology: Images should trigger aspiration for a confident smile and relief that the practice is modern, gentle, and painless.

### Layout
- style: Trust-and-conversion layout with before/after proof, reviews, and easy booking blocks.
- white_space: Moderate
- psychology: Visible proof and simple booking counter the fear and cost-hesitation that keep patients away.

### UI Patterns
- navigation: Sticky bar with prominent "Book appointment" and "Free smile assessment" actions plus phone number.
- cta_style: Reassuring conversion CTAs ("Book your free consult", "Start your smile plan") near financing badges.
- psychology: Pairing low-commitment offers with financing cues neutralizes both fear and price objections at once.

### Copy Style
- tone: Warm, confidence-building, gently persuasive.
- length: Medium
- psychology: Empathy about anxiety and cost, paired with outcomes, converts nervous, deliberating patients.
