# Event Management Doctrine
business_type: events
sector: Other Verticals

## Visual Doctrine

### Colors
- primary: "#2A1A4A"
- secondary: "#F5F1FA"
- accent: "#FF4D8D"
- psychology: Deep midnight-purple signals occasion, drama, and after-dark energy, soft lilac-white conveys elegant polish, and vivid magenta injects celebratory excitement and FOMO.

### Typography
- heading: Playfair Display (Serif)
- body: Montserrat (Sans-serif)
- psychology: Dramatic high-contrast serif headlines feel like a headline act while a clean geometric body reads organized and dependable for logistics-conscious buyers.

### Imagery
- style: Energetic, atmospheric photography capturing crowds, lighting, and peak moments.
- subjects: Packed venues, stage and decor detail, joyful attendees, behind-the-scenes production, and highlight reels.
- psychology: Images should trigger excitement, belonging, and the fear of missing an unforgettable moment.

### Layout
- style: Bold, dynamic layout with strong hero visuals, countdowns, and clear ticketing paths.
- white_space: Moderate
- psychology: High-energy structure with urgency cues mirrors the anticipation and momentum of an event build-up.

### UI Patterns
- navigation: Streamlined top nav (Events, Tickets, About, Contact) with a persistent Get Tickets button and countdown.
- cta_style: Bold, urgent CTAs ("Get Tickets", "Reserve Your Spot") with scarcity indicators like remaining seats.
- psychology: Urgency-driven interaction converts excitement into immediate commitment before hesitation sets in.

### Copy Style
- tone: Energetic, exclusive, urgency-driven.
- length: Short
- psychology: Punchy, FOMO-charged language turns interest into action while momentum and scarcity are highest.
