# Activewear & Athleisure Doctrine
business_type: activewear
sector: Fashion & Beauty

## Visual Doctrine

### Colors
- primary: "#0F1C2E"
- secondary: "#F4F6F8"
- accent: "#2CE5A3"
- psychology: Deep athletic navy conveys performance and discipline, clean light grey feels fresh and breathable, and an energized mint-green pulse signals vitality and progress.

### Typography
- heading: Sora (Sans-serif)
- body: Manrope (Sans-serif)
- psychology: Modern engineered headlines feel high-performance and technical while a humanist body reads warm and motivating for everyday wear.

### Imagery
- style: Dynamic motion photography with sweat, natural light, and fabric-in-motion detail.
- subjects: Diverse real bodies mid-movement, fabric close-ups, training and rest-day lifestyle moments.
- psychology: Images should trigger aspiration toward a stronger self, trust in performance, and belonging to a healthy community.

### Layout
- style: Clean modular grid with fabric-tech callouts, size inclusivity cues, and lifestyle blocks.
- white_space: Moderate
- psychology: Balanced structure communicates both performance credibility and calm everyday comfort.

### UI Patterns
- navigation: Clear category and activity-based nav with a fit and size finder.
- cta_style: Confident rounded buttons ("Shop the Legging", "Find Your Fit") with subtle motion on hover.
- psychology: Guided fit tools reduce anxiety and build trust in a high-return, sizing-sensitive category.

### Copy Style
- tone: Motivational, inclusive, performance-credible.
- length: Medium
- psychology: Empowering language ties the product to identity and progress, not just fabric.
