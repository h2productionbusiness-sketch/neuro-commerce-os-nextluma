# Legal Services Doctrine
business_type: legal
sector: Financial & Legal

## Visual Doctrine

### Colors
- primary: "#1B2A4A"
- secondary: "#F3F1EC"
- accent: "#8C6A3F"
- psychology: Deep authoritative navy signals judgment and gravitas, warm parchment reads as established and trustworthy, and a bronze accent evokes tradition, seals, and enduring counsel.

### Typography
- heading: Freight Display (Serif)
- body: Lora (Serif)
- psychology: Traditional serifs throughout convey precedent, authority, and the weight of the written word that defines the profession.

### Imagery
- style: Composed editorial photography with controlled lighting and architectural gravitas.
- subjects: Confident attorneys, handshake and consultation moments, law-office interiors, and detail shots of documents and books.
- psychology: Images should trigger confidence, protection, and the assurance of a capable advocate in your corner.

### Layout
- style: Structured, hierarchical layout with clear practice-area sections and prominent credentials.
- white_space: Moderate
- psychology: Ordered, formal structure signals rigor and the disciplined handling of high-stakes matters.

### UI Patterns
- navigation: Clear practice-area navigation with an always-visible consultation and contact path.
- cta_style: Confident solid CTAs ("Request a consultation", "Speak to an attorney") with trust signals.
- psychology: Direct, obvious paths to help reduce anxiety for clients under legal stress.

### Copy Style
- tone: Authoritative, reassuring, precise, plain about outcomes.
- length: Medium
- psychology: Clear, confident language turns intimidation into trust that the firm will handle the burden.
