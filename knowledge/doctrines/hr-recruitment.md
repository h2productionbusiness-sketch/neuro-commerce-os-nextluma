# HR & Recruitment Doctrine
business_type: hr-recruitment
sector: B2B & Professional Services

## Visual Doctrine

### Colors
- primary: "#123C69"
- secondary: "#EEF3F8"
- accent: "#2CB1A6"
- psychology: Steady blue conveys reliability and professional trust for hiring decisions, soft light blue-grey feels approachable and human, and a fresh teal accent signals growth, opportunity, and forward momentum.

### Typography
- heading: Poppins (Sans-serif)
- body: Source Sans Pro (Sans-serif)
- psychology: A friendly geometric heading feels modern and human-centric while a highly legible body reads as clear, honest, and easy to trust with a career or a hire.

### Imagery
- style: Warm, authentic workplace photography featuring diverse real people and candid team moments.
- subjects: Diverse professionals collaborating, handshakes, onboarding moments, and candidates in genuine interactions.
- psychology: Images should trigger belonging, trust, and optimism about people and career futures.

### Layout
- style: Clean modular card grid with clear steps, role listings, and human-centered sections.
- white_space: Moderate
- psychology: Orderly, scannable structure reduces friction and anxiety in high-stakes hiring and job-search moments.

### UI Patterns
- navigation: Clear top navigation split between employer and candidate journeys with obvious pathways.
- cta_style: Reassuring solid-button CTAs ("Find Talent", "Get Started", "Submit a Role") with supportive microcopy.
- psychology: Guided, friction-light interaction lowers hesitation for both hiring managers and candidates.

### Copy Style
- tone: Warm, professional, people-first.
- length: Medium
- psychology: Human, empathetic language builds the trust required when careers and company culture are on the line.
