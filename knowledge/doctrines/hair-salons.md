# Hair & Salons Doctrine
business_type: hair-salons
sector: Fashion & Beauty

## Visual Doctrine

### Colors
- primary: "#2B1B2E"
- secondary: "#F4E9E1"
- accent: "#D4A5B8"
- psychology: Deep plum reads as intimate and salon-luxe, warm blush-cream conveys pampering softness, and dusty rose signals feminine transformation and self-care indulgence.

### Typography
- heading: Cormorant Garamond (Serif)
- body: Montserrat (Sans-serif)
- psychology: Elegant high-contrast serifs evoke the boutique-salon feeling of a personal ritual while a clean geometric body keeps booking and pricing effortless to scan.

### Imagery
- style: Bright natural-light lifestyle photography with glossy before-and-after transformation pairings.
- subjects: Fresh cuts and color close-ups, hands styling, satisfied clients in mirror, textured hair detail, and clean salon interiors.
- psychology: Images should trigger aspiration, trust in the stylist's craft, and the anticipation of walking out transformed.

### Layout
- style: Warm editorial grid with prominent gallery and a persistent booking bar.
- white_space: Moderate
- psychology: Balanced spacing feels welcoming and unhurried while keeping the appointment call-to-action always within reach.

### UI Patterns
- navigation: Simple top bar with Book, Services, Gallery, and Team, plus a sticky book-now button.
- cta_style: Warm rounded "Book Now" buttons in accent rose with clear service pricing beside them.
- psychology: Frictionless, reassuring booking flows reduce hesitation and convert the impulse to look better immediately.

### Copy Style
- tone: Warm, personal, confidence-boosting.
- length: Medium
- psychology: Friendly first-person language builds the stylist-client bond that drives loyalty and rebooking.
