1B: The StoryBrand Narrative Framework
1B.1 Objective
To define the brand's role as the guide in the customer's hero journey.
1B.2 Methodology: SB7 Framework + Behavioral Adaptation
STEP 1: Define the Hero (The Customer)
THE CHARACTER QUESTION:
Who is the hero of your story? (Your customer, not you)
What do they want? (Their desire, not your product)
Why do they want it? (The emotional driver)
SOURCE DATA: Phase One - Persona Architecture
OUTPUT: Hero Profile — "The customer is a [description] who wants [desire] because [emotional driver]."

STEP 2: Define the Three Levels of Conflict
LEVEL 1: EXTERNAL PROBLEM
The tangible, physical challenge they face
What's happening to them?
What's the practical problem?
LEVEL 2: INTERNAL PROBLEM
The emotional pain point
How does the external problem make them feel?
What's the psychological tension?
LEVEL 3: PHILOSOPHICAL PROBLEM
The deeper, universal question
Why does this matter beyond the immediate?
What's at stake morally or existentially?
SOURCE DATA: Phase One - Customer Signal Extraction
OUTPUT: Three Levels of Conflict Statement

STEP 3: Define the Guide (The Brand)
1. EMPATHY: "I understand your problem"
You have been there
You understand their struggle
You see them
2. AUTHORITY/COMPETENCE: "I can help you solve it"
You have the expertise
You have the track record
You have the system
SOURCE DATA: Phase One - Brand Authority Assessment + Credentials
OUTPUT: Guide Position Statement — "We understand [empathy] and have [authority]."

STEP 4: Define the Plan
1. PROCESS PLAN: How it works step by step
The customer journey
What to expect
The logical sequence
2. AGREEMENT PLAN: What the customer must do
Their role in the process
What they commit to
What success requires
SOURCE DATA: Phase One - Dual-Path Topography
OUTPUT: Plan Statement — "The process is [steps] and you'll need to [commitment]."

STEP 5: Define the Call to Action
TWO TYPES OF CTA:
1. DIRECT CTA: "Buy Now," "Get Started," "Join Now"
Immediate action
High intent
Clear value
2. TRANSITIONAL CTA: "Learn More," "Download Guide," "Book Demo"
Lower friction
Building trust
Nurturing stage
SOURCE DATA: Phase One - CTA Neurology
OUTPUT: CTA Strategy — "Direct: [X] | Transitional: [Y]"

STEP 6: Define Success (The Transformation)
THE TRANSFORMATION STORY:
Before: The problem state
After: The resolved state
The bridge: How the brand facilitates the transformation
SOURCE DATA: Phase One - Brutal Truth Assessment + Quantum Score
OUTPUT: Transformation Statement
