# PHASE 1 — Discovery Session Guide (90 minutes)

**Owner:** Architect · **Tools:** Zoom/Google Meet, Otter.ai, Claude · **Time:** 2 hrs (90-min session + 30-min processing)
**Outputs:** Transcript, Key Insights, 9W+H tags. Record with client consent.

## Agenda
| Time | Block | Objective |
|------|-------|-----------|
| 0–15 | Welcome & Business Fundamentals | Build rapport, understand the core business |
| 15–30 | Financial Data Collection | Establish the financial baseline |
| 30–45 | Operational Data Collection | Understand day-to-day operations + tech |
| 45–60 | Customer & Market Insights | Deep customer/market understanding |
| 60–75 | Goals & Aspirations | Vision, goals, fears |
| 75–90 | Probing & Clarification | Fill gaps, clarify, confirm |

## Interview Question Bank (by 9W+H dimension)
**A. WHO (Demographics & Identity)** — "Tell me about yourself and your role in the business." · "How would you describe your ideal customer in detail?" · "What communities do you or your customers belong to?"
**B. WHAT (Behaviors & Tasks)** — "Walk me through a typical customer journey from discovery to purchase." · "What specific task does your product solve?" · "What are customers trying to accomplish when they use it?"
**C. WHY (Motivations & Drivers)** — "Why did you start this business?" · "What keeps your customers coming back?" · "What's the deeper reason customers choose you over competitors?"
**D. WHEN (Triggers & Timing)** — "When do customers typically buy? (time of day, season, event)" · "What triggers a customer to start looking for a solution?" · "When do you notice the highest demand?"
**E. WHERE (Environment & Touchpoints)** — "Where do customers discover you?" · "Where do they research alternatives?" · "Where does the transaction actually take place?"
**F. HOW (Process & Decision)** — "How do customers make the purchase decision?" · "How do you currently acquire new customers?" · "How do you deliver value?"
**G. WHICH (Alternatives)** — "Which competitors worry you the most?" · "Which alternatives do customers consider?" · "Which features do customers compare most often?"
**H. WHOSE (Influence)** — "Whose opinion influences the purchase decision?" · "Who else is involved in the decision?" · "Who do your customers trust for advice?"
**I. WISH (Aspirations)** — "What's the ultimate vision for this brand?" · "How do you want customers to feel after using your product?" · "What transformation do you want to create?"

## Probing Question Bank
"Can you tell me more about that?" · "What do you mean by [X]?" · "What have you tried before?" · "What's stopping you from achieving that?" · "How long has this been a challenge?" · "What would success look like for you?" · "If you could wave a magic wand, what would change?" · "What keeps you up at night?" · "What's the one thing you wish you knew earlier?" · "If you could change one thing about your business, what would it be?"

## Block-by-block key questions
- **0–15 (WHO/WHAT/WHICH/WHY):** ONE sentence what you do? · ideal customer? · primary revenue source? · top 3 competitors? · why customers choose you? — *Probe:* "more about that ideal customer?"; "what makes you different?"
- **15–30 (WHAT/HOW):** AOV? · monthly revenue? · monthly marketing spend? · avg customer lifetime? · profit margin? · customers/month? · churn rate? — *Probe:* "How did you calculate that?"; "How has this trended over 12 months?"
- **30–45 (HOW/WHERE/WHAT):** walk me through the customer journey · tools used? · biggest operational headache? · slowest manual processes? · how you generate leads? · how you deliver? · biggest operational fear? — *Probe:* "Show me an example"; "How many hours on that?"
- **45–60 (WHO/WHY/WHERE/WHEN):** ideal customer in 3 words · what keeps them up at night · what they want MOST · where they hang out online · what they read/watch/listen to · what's changing in the industry · biggest complaints — *Probe:* "Why do you think that is?"; "What have customers told you?"
- **60–75 (WISH/WHY):** #1 goal next 12 months · success in 3 months · biggest fear about an agency · the ONE thing that makes it "worth it" · ultimate brand vision · how customers should feel · transformation to create — *Probe:* "What does that look like?"; "What's stopping you?"
- **75–90 (ALL):** the 10 probing questions above — *Close:* "Anything I should have asked but didn't?"; "The most important thing I should know?"
