   - "Walk me through a typical customer journey from discovery to purchase."
   - "What specific task does your product solve for your customer?"
   - "What are your customers trying to accomplish when they use your product?"

   C. WHY Questions (Motivations & Drivers):
   - "Why did you start this business?"

   D. WHEN Questions (Triggers & Timing):

   - "When do you notice the highest customer demand?"

   E. WHERE Questions (Environment & Touchpoints):
   - "Where do customers discover you?"
   - "Where do they research alternatives?"
   - "Where does the transaction actually take place?"

   F. HOW Questions (Process & Decision):
   - "How do customers make the purchase decision?"
   - "How do you currently acquire new customers?"
   - "How do you deliver value to your customers?"

   G. WHICH Questions (Alternatives):
   - "Which competitors worry you the most?"
   - "Which alternatives do customers consider before choosing you?"
   - "Which features do customers compare most often?"

   H. WHOSE Questions (Influence):
   - "Whose opinion influences the purchase decision?"
   - "Who else is involved in the decision-making process?"
   - "Who do your customers trust for advice?"

   I. WISH Questions (Aspirations):
   - "What's the ultimate vision for this brand?"
   - "How do you want customers to feel after using your product?"
   - "What transformation do you want to create for your customers?"
