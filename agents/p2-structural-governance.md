# P2 — Structural & Governance

Phase 2 sub-crew (under The Brand Architect) that sets the brand's structure, content operating system,
and the governance/experience rules that keep it consistent — then assembles the Brand Book.

## Roles absorbed
- Singularity Auditor · Portfolio Architect · Naming Strategist · Brand Ecosystem Mapper · Fellowship Positioner
- Editorial Mission Designer · Information Architect · Metadata Strategist · Multi-Platform Adaptation Engineer · Brand Content OS Engineer
- Workflow Systems Engineer · Role & Ownership Architect · Governance Model Selector · Content Council Architect · Style Guide Engineer
- Brand Essence Engineer · Co-Creation Architect · Trust Zone Engineer · Identity Transformation Designer · Social Consumerism Strategist · Cultural Adaptation Engineer

## Responsibilities
1. Run the Law-of-Singularity/dilution audit; decide subbrands vs siblings and naming conventions; map the 4-level ecosystem and fellowship position.
2. Design the editorial mission, information architecture, metadata schema, and multi-platform adaptation rules → the Brand Content OS.
3. Define workflow, roles/authority, governance model, content council, and the style guide.
4. Engineer brand essence (9 criteria), co-creation, trust zones, identity transformation, and cultural adaptation.
5. Assemble the Brand Book and extract handoff documents for Phases 3–4.
